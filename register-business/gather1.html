Info you need to request a BN

Intro sentence.

Canadian residents

Personal information

You must provide all of the following:

  • Owner's name and phone number

If you are a representative, you will need to provide your RepID.

For details: Represent a Client

RC1 only: If you are a third-party requester, you must also provide:

  • Your name, title, and telephone number

Basic business information

You must provide all of the following:

  • Business name
  • Type of business or organization (for example: sole proprietor, partnership, corporation, domestic worker, trust)
  • Description of major business activity
  • Name and SIN of owners as required
  • Physical address
  • Mailing address (if different from the physical address)
Non-residents

Personal information

You must provide all of the following:

  • Owner's name, title, and phone number
  • Name and address of the primary contact

If you are a third-party requester, you must also provide:

  • Your name, title, and telephone number

Basic business information

You must provide all of the following:

  • Business name
  • Type of business or organization (for example: sole proprietor, partnership, corporation, domestic worker, trust)
  • Description of major business activity in Canada
  • Main products and services provided by the business, with their estimated percentage of revenue
  • Physical address
  • Mailing address (if different from the physical address)

Info you need to register for program accounts

Select the program account you want to add to see what additional information you will need:

GST/HST program account

GST/HST - Canadian residents

You must have all of the following on hand:

  • amount of your annual worldwide and domestic GST/HST taxable supplies
  • fiscal year-end
  • effective date of registration
  • reporting period

You will also need all of the following if it differs from the basic business information above:

  • mailing address
  • physical address

For more information, go to Register for a GST/HST account.

GST/HST - Non-residents

Payroll deductions program account

Payroll - Canadian residents

You must have all of the following on hand:

  • date employees receive their first wages
  • months covered for payroll of employees' wages
  • type of pay period (for example, weekly, quarterly)
  • number of employees
  • payroll service name (if any)
  • country of parent or affiliate, if you have a foreign owned corporation
  • name of franchisor (if any)
    • country of franchise's head office

You will also need all of the following if it differs from the basic business information above:

  • mailing address
  • physical address

For more information, go to Open or manage a payroll account.

Payroll - Non-residents

Corporation income tax program account

Corporation income tax - Canadian residents

You must have all of the following on hand:

  • Corporation name
  • Operation type
  • Date of incorporation
  • Jurisdiction
  • Mailing address
  • Physical address

For more information, go to Corporation income tax program account.

Registraton methods

You can register for this program account in the following ways:

  • Online through Business Registration Online (BRO)
  • By mail with Form RC1, Request for a Business Number and Certain Program Accounts
  • By phone
Corporation income tax - Non-residents

You must have all of the following on hand:

  • Type of business
  • Date and country of incorporation or formation
  • Certificate number
  • Canadian business number (if the business already has one)
  • Will you be filing certain Special Elections or Special Returns?

Registraton methods

You can register for this program account in the following ways:

  • Online webform
  • By mail with Form RC1, Request for a Business Number and Certain Program Accounts

Registered charity program account

Registered charity - Canadian residents

You must have all of the following on hand:

  • type of program account
    • charity
    • Canadian amateur athletic association (CAAA)
    • national arts service organization (NASO)

You will also need all of the following if it differs from the basic business information above:

  • mailing address
  • physical address
  • books and records address

For more information, go to Registering for charitable or other qualified donee status.

Information returns program account

Information returns - Canadian residents

You must have all of the following on hand:

  • type of program account
    • T5 group
    • tax-free savings account (TFSA)
    • T5018
    • partnerships
  • effective date of registration

You will also need all of the following if it differs from the basic business information above:

  • mailing address
  • physical address

For more information, go to Information returns program account.

Information returns - Non-residents
