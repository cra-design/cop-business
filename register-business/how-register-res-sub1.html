Program accounts most businesses need

You can register for any of the following at the same time:

  • Business number (BN)
  • GST/HST (RT)
  • Payroll deductions (RP)
  • Corporation income tax (RC)
  • Registered charity (RR)
  • Information returns (RZ)
  • Luxury Tax (LT)
  • Underused Housing Tax (RU)

Online using Business Registration Online (BRO)

Processing time: Immediate

Things to keep in mind:

  • Your online session times out after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • You cannot save information during the registration process

Register using BRO

Transfer from BRO to Ontario or Nova Scotia provincial sites

After registering for a BN and CRA program accounts using BRO, you can transfer directly from BRO to register for provincial accounts with one of the following:

  • Ontario
  • Nova Scotia

Things to keep in mind:

  • Your BRO session will end once you've been transferred to your provincial registration site

To transfer to your provincial registration site after your federal registration:

  1. Complete your BN and CRA program accounts registration with Register using BRO
  2. Select Transfer to provincial site to connect directly from BRO to your provincial registration site:
    • Ontario - Business services with ServiceOntario
    • Nova Scotia - Registry of Joint Stock Companies

Other ways to register

Operating a business in Quebec

If your business is physically located in Quebec:

  • The GST/HST is administered by Revenu Québec
  • You have to file your returns with Revenu Québec and use their forms
    Exception for Selected Listed Financial Institutions (SLFI)

    If you are a Selected Listed Financial Institution (SLFI) for the GST/HST or Quebec Sales Tax (QST) purposes, or both, there are other forms you may need to fill out with the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).

    For details: GST/HST and QST – Financial institutions, including selected listed financial institutions

For more information:

  • Refer to Revenu Québec - Publication IN-203-V, General information concerning the QST and the GST/HST
  • Contact General information for businesses at Revenu Québec
