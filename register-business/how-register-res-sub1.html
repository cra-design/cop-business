Most program accounts

You can register for any of the following at the same time:

  • Business number (BN)
  • GST/HST (RT)
  • Payroll deductions (RP)
  • Corporation income tax (RC)
  • Registered charity (RR)
  • Information returns (RZ)
  • Luxury Tax (LT)
  • Underused Housing Tax (RU)

Online using Business Registration Online (BRO)

Processing time: Immediate

Things to keep in mind:

  • Your online session times out after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • You cannot save information during the registration process

Register using BRO

Transfer from BRO to Ontario or Nova Scotia provincial sites

After registering for a BN and CRA program accounts using BRO, you can transfer directly from BRO to register for provincial accounts with one of the following:

  • Ontario
  • Nova Scotia

Things to keep in mind:

  • Your BRO session will end once you've been transferred to your provincial registration site

To transfer to your provincial registration site after your federal registration:

  1. Complete your BN and CRA program accounts registration with Register using BRO
  2. Select Transfer to provincial site to connect directly from BRO to your provincial registration site:
    • Ontario - Business services with ServiceOntario
    • Nova Scotia - Registry of Joint Stock Companies

Other ways to register

Registering online is the fastest and simplest way to register. If you are unable to complete your registration using the Business Registration Online (BRO) service, you can either send in a completed form or contact us by phone.

Send a form to the CRA

Processing time: x to x business days

You can complete a form to register for a BN and the following program accounts with the CRA:

  • Business number (BN)
  • GST/HST (RT)
  • Payroll deductions (RP)
  • Corporation income tax (RC)
  • Registered charity (RR)
  • Information returns (RZ)
  • Luxury Tax (LT)

You can not use this form to register for the following:

  • Underused Housing Tax

For all program accounts listed above (except for Luxury Tax and Underused Housing Tax)

  1. Fill out the appropriate sections you want to a register for on Form RC1, Request for a Business Number and Certain Program Accounts:
    • Part A - Business number (BN) if you don't have one
    • Part B - GST/HST (RT)
    • Part C - Payroll deductions (RP)
    • Part D - Corporation income tax (RC)
    • Part E - Information return (RZ)
    • Part F - Registered charity (RR)
  2. Send your completed form to your tax centre (TC)

For Luxury tax registration

  1. Fill out Form L500, Luxury Tax Registration Application to register for:
    • A business number (BN) if you don't have one
    • The Luxury Tax program account (LT)
  2. Fill out additional forms if you want to:
    • Maintain books and records outside Canada: Form L500-1, Non-resident - Records Kept Outside Canada
    • File separate returns for different business branches or divisions: Form L500-2, Application or Revocation of the Authorization to file separate Luxury tax returns for Branches and Divisions
  3. Choose how you want to send your completed forms to the CRA

    • Send a paper form by mail: Option 1

      Processing time: x to x business days

      Mail your completed form to the address on the last page of the form.

    • Submit your forms online using CRA sign in: Option 2

      Processing time: x to x business days

      You must already have a BN and a business account in your CRA account

      1. Sign in to your CRA account and select your Business account
      2. Under "Correspondence," select Submit documents
      3. On the "Submitted documents" page, select Submit documents again
      4. Select the option to upload a document without a case or reference number
      5. Choose Luxury Tax, Fuel Charge, Excise Duty, Excise Tax and ATSC from the drop-down list
      6. Select Submit Luxury Tax registration forms and related documents
      7. Select the associated program account number
      8. Choose and attach your saved form

Excise and specialty taxes

You can register for a BN and the following CRA program accounts:

  • Excise duty (RD)
  • Excise tax and special levies (RE)
  • Air travelers security charge (RG)
  • Insurance premium tax (RN)
Excise duty program account (RD) registration

You must have a business number (BN) before you can register for an excise duty program account (RD). To get a BN, refer to the section above Program accounts for most businesses.

Operating a business in Quebec

If your business is physically located in Quebec:

  • The GST/HST is administered by Revenu Québec
  • You have to file your returns with Revenu Québec and use their forms
    Exception for Selected Listed Financial Institutions (SLFI)

    If you are a Selected Listed Financial Institution (SLFI) for the GST/HST or Quebec Sales Tax (QST) purposes, or both, there are other forms you may need to fill out with the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).

    For details: GST/HST and QST – Financial institutions, including selected listed financial institutions

For more information:

  • Refer to Revenu Québec - Publication IN-203-V, General information concerning the QST and the GST/HST
  • Contact General information for businesses at Revenu Québec
