Register online

Registering online is the fastest and easiest way to register for your business number (BN) and program accounts.

Business Registration Online (BRO)

Processing time: Immediate

Using Business Registration Online (BRO) is the simplest way to complete your registration.

In the same registration session, you can use this option to get:

  • GST/HST (RT)
  • Payroll deductions (RP)
  • Corporation income tax (RC)
  • Registered charity (RR)
  • Information returns (RZ)
  • Underused Housing Tax (RU)
  • Luxury Tax (LT)

When you register, remember to:

  • Enter your information in a timely manner because the form will time out after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • Complete the form in one session because you cannot save information during the registration process

You can enter BRO through one of the following methods:

Business owners or third-party requesters
BRO quick link
You will have to add your SIN and other identifying information
Business owners
Sign in to your CRA account
On the Welcome page, select Add account, then select Business account, then select Business Registration Online
Representatives with a RepID
Enter BRO through Represent a Client in your CRA account.

Your CRA account

Processing time: x to x business days

If you already have a BN attached to your CRA sign in, you can submit forms for the following program accounts in your CRA account:

  • Luxury tax (LT)
  • Fuel charge (CT)
  • Excise duty (RD)
  • Excise tax and special levies (RE)
  • Air travelers security charge (RG)
Submit a form in your CRA account
  1. Sign in to your CRA account
  2. Select your Business account to access My Business Account
  3. Complete the necessary form(s)
  4. Under "Correspondence," select Submit documents
  5. On the "Submitted documents" page, select Submit documents again
  6. Select the option to upload a document without a case or reference number
  7. Choose Luxury Tax, Fuel Charge, Excise Duty, Excise Tax and ATSC from the drop-down list
  8. Indicate the reason for your submission
  9. Select the associated program account number
  10. Choose and attach your saved form
