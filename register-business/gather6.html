Steps for gathering what you need to register

Intro sentence.

  1. Gather some basic information

    Regardless of what you are registering for, you may need to gather the following:

    • Owner's name, title and phone number
    • Owner's social insurance number (SIN) or temporary tax number (TTN)
    • Name and address of the primary contact
    • Business name
    • Type of business or organization
    • Description of the business operations
    • Main products and services provided by the business, with their estimated percentage of revenue
    • Mailing, physical, and books and records addresses
    • Effective date of registration for any program account you are adding
    • Corporation date, jurisdiction and certificate number, if applicable
    • Business number, if the business already has one
      What to do if you have lost your business number

      Go to Find an existing business number

  2. Get additional information based on the program account you need

    GST/HST program account

    You must have all of the following on hand:

    • Amount of your annual worldwide and domestic GST/HST taxable income
    • Fiscal year-end
    • Reporting period

    If you are a non-resident, you will also need:

    • Type of products or services the business collects GST/HST on and supplier type (if applicable)
    • Filing type (simplified or normal)
    • Banking information
    • Information about Canadian employees (if applicable)
    • Address of location branch or office in Canada (if applicable)
    Payroll deductions program account

    You must have all of the following on hand:

    • Months of operation
    • Number of employees
    • Expected annual employee salaries
    • Date employees receive their first wages
    • Frequency and type of payments
    • Payroll service name (if any)
    • Affiliate, subsidiary, or franchise information (if applicable)
    Corporation income tax program account

    You must have all of the following on hand:

    • Operation type
    • Corporation name

    If you are a non-resident, you will also have to know if you will be filing certain special elections or special returns.

    Registered charity program account

    You must have all of the following on hand:

    • Type of account needed:
      • Charity
      • Canadian amateur athletic association (CAAA)
      • National arts service organization (NASO)
    Information returns program account - Canadian residents

    You must know the type of account needed:

    • T5 group
    • Tax-free savings account (TFSA)
    • Contract payments
    • Compliance and exchange
    Non-residents - Information returns program account

    You must know the type of account needed:

    • T5 group
    • Tax-free savings account (TFSA)
    • T5018
    • Partnerships
    • Compliance and exchange

    You must also know if you will be filing certain special elections or special returns.
