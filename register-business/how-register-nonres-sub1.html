Business number (BN) and most program accounts

Registering online is the simplest way to complete most registrations.

Non-resident webform

Processing time: Info

Use this webform to get a BN and the following program accounts:

  • GST/HST for digital economy - normal or simplified
  • Payroll
  • Information returns
  • Corporation
  • UHT (corporations only)

Things to keep in mind:

  • Your online session times out after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • You cannot save information during the registration process

Non-res webform

Other ways to register

If you can't register online with the non-resident webform, you can use one of the following methods:

By mail

Processing time: Info

Use Form RC1 - Request for a Business Number and Certain Program Accounts to register for a BN and the following program accounts:

  • GST/HST for digital economy - normal only
  • Payroll
  • Information returns
  • Corporation

You can not use this form to register for the following:

  • GST/HST for digital economy - simplified
  • Underused Housing Tax

Print out the completed form and send it to a non-resident tax centre.

By phone

Processing time: Info

If you already have a business number, you can add the following program accounts by phone:

  • Payroll
  • Information returns
  • UHT (if you are not a corporation)
  • Payroll without a BN
  • Information returns without a BN
  • GST/HST for digital economy - normal or simplified
  • Corporation
  • Underused Housing Tax (if you are a corporation)

Digital services tax

Processing time: Info

Use this webform to get the Digital services tax (DT) program account.

Register for the Digital services tax program account

You must provide your business number if you already have one.

If you don't already have a business number, you will receive one.

Things to keep in mind:

  • Your online session times out after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • You cannot save information during the registration process

Luxury tax program account

Fill out Form L500, Luxury Tax Registration Application

You may also need to fill out one or more of the following:

  • L500-1, Non-Resident – Records Kept Outside Canada
  • L500-2, Application or Revocation of the Authorization to File Separate Luxury Tax Returns for Branches and Divisions
Submit your form online if you have a business number

Processing time: Info

If you already have a business number and have added a business account to your CRA account, you can submit this registration form online.

  1. Sign in to your CRA account
  2. Select your Business account to access My Business Account
  3. Under "Correspondence," select Submit documents
  4. On the "Submitted documents" page, select Submit documents again
  5. Select the option to upload a document without a case or reference number
  6. Choose Luxury Tax, Fuel Charge, Excise Duty, Excise Tax and ATSC from the drop-down list
  7. Select Submit Luxury Tax registration forms and related documents
  8. Select the associated program account number
  9. Choose and attach your saved form
Mail in your form if you need a business number

Processing time: Info

If you don't already have a business number, you must also complete Form RC1

Send your completed forms to the appropriate address listed on the last page of the form.
