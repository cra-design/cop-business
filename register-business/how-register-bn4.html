Choose the method that works best for you

Choose one of the following ways to register for:

  • Business number
  • GST/HST
Business Registration Online

Processing time: Immediate

This is the simplest way to complete your registration.

You can also use this option to get a business number and add certain other program accounts in the same registration session.

What you can do in BRO at the same time

You can do the following at the same time in BRO:

  • Get a business number
  • Add one or more of the following program accounts:
    • GST/HST
    • Payroll deductions
    • Corporation income tax
    • Registered charity
    • Information returns
    • Luxury tax
    • Underused housing tax

When you register, remember to:

  • Enter your information in a timely manner because the form will time out after 30 minutes of inactivity
    • to get a
  • Complete the form in one session because you cannot save information during the registration process

If you're a business owner

You can enter BRO through one of the following methods:

  • BRO quick link: Option 1

    You will have to add your SIN and other identifying information

    Register now

  • Your CRA account: Option 2

    On the Welcome page, select Add account, then select Business account, then select Business Registration Online

    Sign in to your CRA account

If you're a representative with a RepID

Enter BRO through Represent a Client in your CRA account.

By mail

Processing time: Info

  1. Fill out Form L500, Luxury Tax Registration Application
    If you also need a business number, fill out Form RC1, Request for a Business Number and Certain Program Accounts
    Additional forms you may need to fill out when registering

    If you would like to apply to keep books and records outside Canada, an authorization under subsection 88(3) of the Select Luxury Items Tax Act may be granted to maintain such records outside Canada. To request this authorization, you must fill out form L500-1, Non-Resident – Records Kept Outside Canada. 

    If you would like to apply to file separate returns for different branches or divisions of your business under subsection 73(1) of the Select Luxury Items Tax Act, you must fill out form L500-2, Application or Revocation of the Authorization to File Separate Luxury Tax Returns for Branches and Divisions

  2. Send your completed forms to the appropriate address listed on the last page of the forms.
