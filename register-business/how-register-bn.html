Choose a register method

How you registers depends on whether you are a:

  • Residents of Canada
  • Non-residents - Outside of Canada

The fastest and easiest way to register is online with one of the web forms, or you can choose one of the other methods.

Residents of Canada

Online with web form - BN and some program accounts

How to register by online web form

You can use BRO to get a BN and also register for one or more of these Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) program accounts:

  • GST/HST program account (RT)
  • Payroll deductions program account (RP)
  • Corporation income tax program account (RC)
  • Registered charity program account (RR)
  • Information returns (RZ)
  • Luxury tax program account (LT)
  • Underused housing tax program account (RU)

Once you complete registering using this option, you will immediately receive your new nine-digit BN. You must keep this new BN for your own records and future interactions with the CRA.

Register using BRO – What you can do

If you're a representative with a RepID, first sign in to Represent a Client service and use BRO through it.

Using the paper form

How to register by form

You can use the Form RC1, Request for a Business Number and Certain Program Accounts, or order the form through Forms and publications.

Fill in Form RC1. Once you have completed the form, mail or fax it to your nearest tax services office or tax centre. To find the appropriate office or centre, go to Find a CRA address.

By phone

How to register by phone

Before calling, be ready to answer all the questions in Part A of Form RC1, Request for a Business Number and Certain Program Accounts, and any other questions in the form about the CRA program accounts you want to open.

From Canada or the United States

  • 1-800-959-5525 (any province or the United States)
  • 1-866-841-1876 (Yukon, Northwest Territories or Nunavut)

Hours of service for both numbers:

  • Monday to Friday: 6:30 am to 11 pm ET
  • Saturday: 7:30 am to 8:00 pm ET
  • Sunday: Closed

Closed on public holidays.

Businesses operating in Quebec

How to register for businesses operating in Quebec

In Quebec, Revenu Québec generally administers the GST/HST. If the physical location of your business is in Quebec, you have to file your returns with Revenu Québec using its forms, unless you are a person that is a selected listed financial institution (SLFI) for GST/HST or QST purposes or both. For more information, see the Revenu Québec publication IN-203-V, General Information Concerning the QST and the GST/HST, available at Revenu Québec, or call 1-800-567-4692. If you are an SLFI, go to GST/HST and QST – Financial institutions, including selected listed financial institutions.

Non-residents - Outside of Canada

Online with web form - Non-resident BN and some program accounts

How to register by online web form

This service is available for non-resident businesses that need to register for a BN.

This service is the only registration option available to non-resident digital economy businesses who are required to register for the simplified GST/HST. Go to Find out if you need to register for the GST/HST: GST/HST for digital economy businesses.

You can also use this service to register for one or more of these CRA program accounts:

  • GST/HST program account (RT)
  • Payroll deductions program account (RP)
  • Corporation income tax program account (RC)
  • Information returns (RZ)

What you need to know before using this web form:

  • Session information cannot be saved
  • Registration must be completed in full each time
  • This form will time out after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • Do not use the "Back" button in your web browser, but use the "Previous" button instead

For non-resident digital economy businesses registering for the GST/HST, go to Get ready to register for the GST/HST for a list of information you will need.

Register using the non-resident registration web form

Note: If you are an individual with a social insurance number that starts with the number 9 and want to register for a BN or a GST/HST account, go to Business Registration Online.

Online with web form - Non-resident Underused Housing Tax

This service is the only registration option available to non-resident corporations looking to create an Underused Housing Tax (UHT) account. You cannot create other CRA program accounts using this web form.

What you need to know before using this web form:

  • Session information cannot be saved
  • Registration must be completed in full each time
  • This form will time out after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • Do not use the "Back" button in your web browser, but use the "Previous" button instead
