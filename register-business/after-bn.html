Changes to a registered business

Business changes in ownership or structure

If you already have a BN and you want to change the legal ownership or the structure of your business, you may have to register for a new BN.

For details: Changes to your business and CRA program accounts

Bankruptcy

There is also an exception in the case of a sole proprietor in bankruptcy.

For details: Bankruptcy – Business structures

Transfering to a provincial partner

After getting your BN and registering for CRA program accounts through BRO, you can transfer to one of the provinces listed below.

Before you click the Transfer to provincial site button, make sure you have registered for all the program accounts you need because your session in BRO will end once you connect to one of the provinces.

For details:

  • Ontario – ServiceOntario – Business services
  • Nova Scotia – Registry of Joint Stock Companies
