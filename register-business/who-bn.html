What is BRO

Business Registration Online (BRO) is a secure, online service that lets you quickly get a business number (BN) and register for some types of Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) program accounts.

You can use BRO to:

  • Get a BN
  • Register for some typoes of program accounts
  • Link to other business online registries for some provincial programs, such as Ontario and Nova Scotia

If you want to register your business as a non-resident business, go to Non-Resident Business Number and Account Registration Web Form.

Available CRA program accounts

If you already have a BN, you can use BRO to register for some types of program accounts.

If you use BRO to get a BN, you can register for these accounts in the same session.

You can use BRO to register for the followig types of program accounts:

  • Corporation income tax
  • GST/HST
  • Payroll deductions
  • Import-export
  • Registered charity
  • Information returns
  • Underused housing tax

For information about other CRA program accounts that are not found in BRO, go to Other CRA program accounts.

BRO and provincial partners

After getting your BN and registering for one or more CRA program accounts through BRO, you can transfer to one of the provinces listed below.

Before you click the Transfer to provincial site button, make sure you have registered for all the program accounts you need because your session in BRO will end once you connect to one of the provinces.
