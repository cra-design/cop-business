Get ready to register

Gather your information ahead of time because your session will time out.

On this page

  • Info everyone needs to gather
  • Info you need to register for a BN
  • Info you need to register for program accounts

Info everyone needs to gather

Regardless of what you are registering for, you will need to gather:

  • Owner's name
  • Business name
  • Description of the business operations
  • Mailing and physical address

Info you need to register for a BN

Canadian residents

In addition to the info everyone must gather, you must also provide the following:

  • Owner's phone number
  • Type of business or organization (for example: sole proprietor, partnership, corporation, domestic worker, trust)
Non-residents

You must provide all of the following:

  • Owner's name, title, and phone number
  • Name and address of the primary contact
  • If you are a third-party requester, you must also provide your name, title, and telephone number
  • Business name
  • Type of business or organization (for example: sole proprietor, partnership, corporation, domestic worker, trust)
  • Corporation date, jurisdiction and certificate number, if applicable
  • Description of major business activity in Canada
  • Main products and services provided by the business, with their estimated percentage of revenue
  • Mailing and physical address

Info you need to register for program accounts

If you already have a BN, you will need to validate it. Make sure you have the following information ready:

  • Your business number
  • The owner's SIN
  • The corporation's jurisdiction, if application

If the mailing and physical address is different from the basic business address, you will need to have that ready, as well.

You will also need to provide information specific to the program account you want to add.

Most common program accounts

GST/HST program account

GST/HST - Canadian residents

You must have all of the following on hand:

  • Amount of your annual worldwide and domestic GST/HST taxable income
  • Fiscal year-end
  • Reporting period
  • Effective date of registration
GST/HST - Non-residents

Payroll deductions program account

Payroll - Canadian residents

You must have all of the following on hand:

  • Months of operation
  • Number of employees
  • Expected annual employee salaries
  • Date employees receive their first wages
  • Type of pay period (for example, weekly, quarterly)
  • Payroll service name (if any)
  • Affiliate or franchise information (if applicable)

For more information, go to Open or manage a payroll account.

Payroll - Non-residents

Corporation income tax program account

Corporation income tax - Canadian residents

You must have all of the following on hand:

  • Owner's role type
  • Operation type
  • Corporation name
  • Date of incorporation
  • Jurisdiction
Corporation income tax - Non-residents

You must have all of the following on hand:

  • Type of business
  • Date and country of incorporation or formation
  • Certificate number
  • Canadian business number (if the business already has one)
  • Will you be filing certain Special Elections or Special Returns?

Registraton methods

You can register for this program account in the following ways:

  • Online webform
  • By mail with Form RC1, Request for a Business Number and Certain Program Accounts

Registered charity program account

Registered charity - Canadian residents

You must have all of the following on hand:

  • Type of account needed:
    • Charity
    • Canadian amateur athletic association (CAAA)
    • National arts service organization (NASO)
  • Books and records address

Information returns program account

Information returns - Canadian residents

You must have all of the following on hand:

  • Type of account needed
    • T5 group
    • Tax-free savings account (TFSA)
    • Contract payments
    • Compliance and exchange
  • Effective date of registration
Information returns - Non-residents

  • Books and records address

  • Type of item you are selling or importing
  • Books and records address
