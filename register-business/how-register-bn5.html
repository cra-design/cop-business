Register online

Using Business Registration Online (BRO) is the simplest way to complete your registration.

In the same registration session, you can use this option to get:

  • A business number
  • Add a GST/HST account
  • Registered charity

When you register, remember to:

  • Enter your information in a timely manner because the form will time out after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • Complete the form in one session because you cannot save information during the registration process

If you're a business owner

You can enter BRO through one of the following methods:

  • BRO quick link: Option 1

    You will have to add your SIN and other identifying information

    Register now

  • Your CRA account: Option 2

    On the Welcome page, select Add account, then select Business account, then select Business Registration Online

    Sign in to your CRA account

If you're a representative with a RepID

Enter BRO through Represent a Client in your CRA account.

Other registration methods

If you can't use BRO, you can use one of the following methods.

By mail

Processing time: Info

You can use this option to get:

  • A business number
  • Add a GST/HST account
  • Registered charity
  1. Fill out Form RC1, Luxury Tax Registration Application
  2. Send your completed forms to the appropriate address listed on the last page of the forms.
By phone

Processing time: Info

You can use this option to get:

  • A business number
  • Add a GST/HST account

You can not use this option to get:

  • Registered charity
