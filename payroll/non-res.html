Register as a non-resident doing business in Canada

You may be looking for: Register as a Canadian resident

Registering online is the fastest and easiest way to register for a business number (BN) and a payroll account.

Non-resident Business Registration online webform

You can use the Non-resident Business Registration webform to register for a payroll account. If you don't already have a business number (BN), you will get one. You can also register for certain other program accounts in the same session.

Register for multiple program accounts at the same time

You can register for any of the following with the webform at the same time:

  • Payroll
  • GST/HST for digital economy - normal or simplified
  • Information returns
  • Corporation
  • Underused Housing Tax (corporations only)

Things to keep in mind:

  • Your online session times out after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • You cannot save information during the registration process

Non-resident Business Registration

Information a requestor must provide

The person submitting the registration must provide their:

  • Name
  • Title
  • Work telephone number

Other ways to register

If you can't register online with the non-resident webform, you can use one of the following methods:

By mail

You can register for a BN and the following program accounts by mail on the same form:

  • Payroll
  • GST/HST for digital economy - normal only
  • Information returns
  • Corporation

To register:

  1. Fill out the appropriate sections you want to register for on Form RC1 - Request for a Business Number and Certain Program Accounts
  2. Send your completed form to a non-resident tax centre
By phone

If you already have a business number, you can add the following program accounts by phone:

  • Payroll
  • Information returns
  • Underused Housing Tax (if you are not a corporation)

Before calling

Be ready to provide answers for:

  • Part A of Form RC1, Request for a Business number and certain program accounts
  • Any other sections of the form related to the CRA program accounts you are registering for
