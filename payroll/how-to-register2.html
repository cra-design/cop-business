How to register

Registering online is the fastest and easiest way to register for a payroll account.

On this page

  • Residents with a Canadian business
  • Non-residents doing business in Canada

Residents with a Canadian business

Business Registration Online

You can use Business Registration Online (BRO) register for a payroll account. If you don't already have a business number (BN), you will get one through BRO. You can also register for certain other program accounts in the same session.

Register for multiple program accounts at the same time

You can register for any of the following in BRO at the same time:

  • Business number (BN)
  • Payroll deductions (RP)
  • GST/HST (RT) excluding Quebec
  • Corporation income tax (RC)
  • Registered charity (RR)
  • Information returns (RZ)
  • Luxury Tax (LT)
  • Underused Housing Tax (RU)

Things to keep in mind:

  • You must save or print your BN for your records, it won't be sent to you
  • Your online session times out after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • You cannot save information during the registration process
  • You can transfer directly to Ontario and Nova Scotia registration sites
    • Residents of Ontario and Nova Scotia

    After registering for a BN and CRA program accounts in BRO, you can transfer directly to the provincial registration site. Your BRO session will end once you select Transfer to provincial site.

Ways to access BRO

Choose one of the following options:

Through your CRA account
Sign in with CRA Account Register

For business owners:

  1. Select Add account, then Business account
  2. Select Business Registration Online

For representatives with a RepID, you can access BRO through Represent a Client.

Direct link
Business Registration Online
Information a requester must provide

The person submitting the registration must provide their:

  • Last name
  • Social insurance number (SIN)
  • Date of birth
  • Postal code of their home address

Other ways to register

Registering online is the fastest way to register. If you are unable to complete your registration using BRO, you can register by mail or by phone.

By mail

You can register for a BN and the following CRA program accounts by mail on the same form:

  • Payroll deductions (RP)
  • GST/HST (RT)
  • Corporation income tax (RC)
  • Registered charity (RR)
  • Information returns (RZ)

To register:

  1. Fill out the appropriate sections you want to register for on Form RC1, Request for a Business Number and Certain Program Accounts
  2. Send your completed form to your tax centre
By phone

You can register for a BN and the following CRA program accounts by phone:

  • Payroll deductions (RP)
  • GST/HST (RT)
  • Corporation income tax (RC)
  • Information returns (RZ)
  • Underused Housing Tax

Before calling

Be ready to provide answers for:

  • Part A of Form RC1, Request for a Business number and certain program accounts
  • Any other sections of the form related to the CRA program accounts you want to register for

Non-residents doing business in Canada

Non-resident Business Registration online webform

You can use the Non-resident Business Registration webform to register for a payroll account. If you don't already have a business number (BN), you will get one. You can also register for certain other program accounts in the same session.

Register for multiple program accounts at the same time

You can register for any of the following with the webform at the same time:

  • Payroll
  • GST/HST for digital economy - normal or simplified
  • Information returns
  • Corporation
  • Underused Housing Tax (corporations only)

Things to keep in mind:

  • Your online session times out after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • You cannot save information during the registration process

Non-resident Business Registration

Information a requestor must provide

The person submitting the registration must provide their:

  • Name
  • Title
  • Work telephone number

Other ways to register

If you can't register online with the non-resident webform, you can use one of the following methods:

By mail

You can register for a BN and the following program accounts by mail on the same form:

  • Payroll
  • GST/HST for digital economy - normal only
  • Information returns
  • Corporation

To register:

  1. Fill out the appropriate sections you want to register for on Form RC1 - Request for a Business Number and Certain Program Accounts
  2. Send your completed form to a non-resident tax centre
By phone

If you already have a business number, you can add the following program accounts by phone:

  • Payroll
  • Information returns
  • Underused Housing Tax (if you are not a corporation)

Before calling

Be ready to provide answers for:

  • Part A of Form RC1, Request for a Business number and certain program accounts
  • Any other sections of the form related to the CRA program accounts you are registering for
